Who is Flor


Nice to meet you! My name is Elaisa, better known as Dona Moça or, simply, Flor. Comidas e Sabores da Flor is the result of a recipe with two main ingredients: the satisfaction I find in cooking and the pride of my Brazilian and Rio de Janeiro origins.


Cooking always aroused my curiosity and brought me enormous pleasure. After living in Brazil and Italy, in 2005 I came to Finland, and I had no doubts about the profession I was going to pursue. During the years of the vocational school, I kept combining the techniques I was learning with the experimentation of new recipes at home.

I have always seen the kitchen space as a great laboratory, where each dish is second to none and an opportunity to express my identity. Around 2016, what had just started serving family and friends, became a greater purpose, the one of providing the Brazilian community in the Finnish capital with the flavors from the homeland.

Currently, Comidas e Sabores da Flor continues to develop with each order. My clients - which I affectionately call Flor'mores - are served with the same love and dedication I serve my own family. In addition to Brazilian meals, which are my trademark, I recently started venturing into confectionary, due to the clients’ requests.

Confectionary is an aspect that I love and that I constantly educate myself about. In this area, it inspires me to know that I am the only granddaughter to follow in the footsteps of my late grandfather, who was a chef at Confeitaria Colombo, in Rio de Janeiro


For me, cooking is synonymous of affection and, therefore, I prefer to follow my intuition than to reproduce ready-made recipes. Something that gives me pleasure is to meet the challenges launched by friends and clients. For this reason, I also experiment with Italian, Thai, Indian and Finnish cuisine.

Today, I feel very fulfilled. I have regular Brazilian clients, as well as other nationalities, interested in the flavors of my country. I live with my husband, Italian, and my pre-teen son in Helsinki, and I would not trade that reality for anything in this world.


I still have the dream of transforming Comidas e Sabores da Flor into my own establishment and serving customers not only with delicious food, but with a good chat, showered with laughter and conviviality.
