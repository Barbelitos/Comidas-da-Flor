Our Work


At Comidas e Sabores da Flor, our mission is to bring the real Brazilian flavor to your home. Made with the best products and seasoning, and with a countless pinches of affection and dedication, Comidas e Sabores da Flor challenges itself a little more every day to provide its customers with a tasty and cozy homemade food.


We offer a variety of individualized services: warm dishes of typical food (quentinhas), Brazilian tapas (petiscos), traditional Brazilian desserts and snacks (salgadinhos) and also confectionary! In addition, we take great pleasure in facing any culinary challenge that our customers propose to us and we offer the Chef at home service, for an unparalleled Brazilian celebration in your special events!


Welcome and see samples of our work below. Contact us by phone or Facebook to place your order and taste our dishes. We deliver in Helsinki, Espoo and Vantaa.
